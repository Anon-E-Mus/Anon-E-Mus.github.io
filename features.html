Raudra RPG - Strategic TCG Card Battler with Deep Progression

Experience a feature-rich mythological card game combining strategic deck building, fair gacha mechanics, guild wars, and competitive PvP arena battles.

Strategic Card Combat System

Turn-Based TCG Battles

Engage in tactical turn-based card combat where strategy matters more than luck. Build powerful decks with deity cards, activate devastating skills, and outsmart your opponents.

  • Turn-based auto-battle with strategic team composition
  • 4 unique skills per deity (Passive, Skill 1, Skill 2, Ultimate)
  • 14 elemental types with strength and weakness interactions
  • Tier-based card power (UR, SSR, SR, R, Uncommon, Common)
  • Real-time PvP arena for competitive card battles
