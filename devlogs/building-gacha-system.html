Building Our Gacha System - Fair & Secure

When we set out to build Raudra Cosmos, we knew the gacha system would be make-or-break. Players need to trust that their pulls are fair, and we needed to build something that's both exciting and transparent. Here's how we approached it.

Why Server-Authoritative Gacha Matters

Many games handle gacha RNG on the client side for simplicity. But that opens the door to manipulation. We made the decision early on to handle all randomness server-side. Every summon request goes through our backend, where the outcome is determined using cryptographically secure random number generation.

The Trust Factor: Players can trust the results because the rates we show are the rates they get. No manipulation, no hidden modifiers.

This approach means every pull is verifiable and consistent. Players never have to wonder if someone found an exploit or if the system is rigged against them.

The Dual Pity System

One of our key design decisions was implementing a dual pity system that balances excitement with fairness:

  • Soft Pity (after 40 pulls): Rates gradually increase for higher-tier deities
  • Hard Pity (at 100 pulls): Guaranteed UR for featured banners

The beauty of this system is its transparency. Players can see exactly how many pulls they've made, and they know when their guaranteed pull is coming. No more questioning "is the pity really at 100 pulls?" - it's all visible and trackable in the UI.

Featured Banners & Rate-Up Events

Our featured banner system gives players a reason to get excited about specific deities. During events, certain deities get a rate-up, meaning they appear more frequently than usual. But we balanced this carefully - other deities are still possible, just less likely.

We also implemented event-only banners where the pity counter is separate from standard banners. This lets players save their progress for the banners they care about most, without losing progress on regular summons.

The Friendship System - F2P Friendly

Not everyone wants to spend money, and that's okay. Our friendship summon system gives free-to-play players a meaningful path to acquiring cards. By sending hearts to friends and participating in co-op content, players can earn friendship points and summon for duplicates - essential for progression.

Daily Limits: Friendship summons have daily caps to maintain game balance while still providing a steady path for dedicated F2P players.

Crypto-Secure RNG

At the heart of our system is cryptographically secure random number generation. We use industry-standard RNG algorithms that provide:

  • Unpredictable outcomes that cannot be reverse-engineered
  • Uniform distribution across all possible results
  • Protection against timing attacks and manipulation

What Makes Our System Different

Building a fair gacha system isn't just about good math - it's about building trust with your players. Here's what sets Raudra Cosmos apart:

  • Transparent rates: What you see is what you get
  • No hidden mechanics: All modifiers are visible to players
  • Player-first design: Systems designed to reward, not exploit
  • Server security: All rolls happen server-side with proper validation

What's Next

We're constantly refining our gacha system based on feedback and testing. Future improvements include:

  • Enhanced pity visualization in the summon UI
  • Detailed summon history tracking
  • Special event tickets with unique mechanics
  • Community-driven rate-up polls

Building a gacha system that players can trust is an ongoing commitment, not a one-time implementation. And that's a commitment we make every day.
